💡 `gordian tests` needed namespace provenance (`:src` vs `:test`) preserved at scan time; `--include-tests` alone was not enough. Once src and test namespaces were merged into a plain `{ns -> deps}` graph, test-specific analysis became impossible or heuristic-only. The enabling refactor was: typed discovery paths → provenance-aware scan (`:origins`) → derive both `src-only` and `src+test` views from one scan. Future architecture modes that compare slices of the codebase should preserve origin/segment metadata early rather than trying to reconstruct it later from namespace names.

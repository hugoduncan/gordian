💡 Test architecture analysis needs a first-class distinction between executable test namespaces and shared test support namespaces. Treating all test namespaces with incoming deps as violations creates noisy false positives: support namespaces may validly be reused by tests, while executable tests should usually remain leaves. The sharper signals are: `src -> test` leakage, support namespaces depended on by src, and executable tests with incoming deps. For v1, a name-based support heuristic was good enough; future refinements can inspect test forms directly.
